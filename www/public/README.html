Public JSON files

committee-info.json
Committee (PMC) names and membership (documentation)
icla-info.json
Names of people who have submitted ICLAs who have uids (committer accounts) (documentation)
icla-info_noid.json
Names of people who have submitted ICLAs who don't have uids. Either the ICLA has yet to be processed, or it was submitted and not followed up. (documentation)
member-info.json
ASF member names (documentation)
public_ldap_authgroups.json
Membership of LDAP auth groups. Membership of an LDAP auth group is used to grant commit karma for sub-project and other such resources (documentation)
public_ldap_committees.json
Membership of LDAP committee groups. Membership of an LDAP committee group != membership of the PMC. (documentation)
Will eventually be replaced by public_ldap_projects.json
public_ldap_groups.json
Membership of LDAP unix groups. Membership of an LDAP unix group is used to grant commit karma for that project. (documentation)
Will eventually be replaced by public_ldap_projects.json
public_ldap_people.json
Basic details of LDAP personal entries. Shows the public name and whether the login is currently disabled. (documentation)
public_ldap_projects.json
Details of PMCs and Podlings. The "owners" list holds the ids with (P)PMC karma, and the "members" list holds the ids with (P)PMC committer karma. (documentation)
Currently being introduced; will eventually replace public_ldap_groups and public_ldap_committees
public_ldap_services.json
Membership of LDAP service groups. For example pmc_chairs shows the list of people with PMC chair karma (documentation)
public_nonldap_groups.json
Membership of auth groups not defined in LDAP. For example, podlings. (documentation)
Podlings are being moved to public_ldap_projects.json, this file is likely to become obsolete.
public_podling_status.json
Status of podlings: current, graduated, retired (documentation)
public_podlings.json
podling details from https://svn.apache.org/repos/asf/incubator/public/trunk/content/podlings.xml (documentation)
site-scan.json
Data for Website link checks. (documentation)
